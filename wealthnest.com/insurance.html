Client fund insurance upto $1,000,000.00

Our Client Fund Insurance offers protection of up to $1,000,000.00 ensuring your assets are secure against unforeseen risks. With this coverage, you can trade and invest with confidence, knowing your funds are protected every step of the way.

What is WealthNest insurance coverage?

Trade insurance coverage protects businesses from financial losses due to client non-payment, ensuring cash flow and reducing bad debt risks. Covering both domestic and international transactions, it provides security in uncertain markets, allowing businesses to grow confidently while extending credit. We have added additional protection on your funds so you can continue trading with more confidence. The insurance will cover up to $1,000,000 per client.

What does the insurance coverage include?

Trade insurance covers both the client's available balance and open CFD positions, protecting businesses from losses due to client non-payment. This comprehensive coverage ensures security for funds and active trades, reducing financial risk and safeguarding your business.

Can I benefit from the insurance coverage?

The Insurance Coverage is available to all the clients of Trade StratIQ, and will be triggered only in the unlikely event of Trade StratIQ's insolvency.
